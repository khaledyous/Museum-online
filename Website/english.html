WHAT CAN YOU DO HERE?

The Nxt Museum is a dynamic space that regularly changes exhibitions. As a result, Nxt Amsterdam always offers exciting experiences for every visitor, even on a second, third and fourth visit to the museum! The museum takes you on a digital journey full of spectacles again and again. To stay up-to-date on current exhibitions, we recommend that you consult the website before your visit. This way you know exactly what to expect when visiting Nxt Museum Amsterdam. In addition to standard digital art, Nxt Amsterdam also offers fun workshops. Of course you visit the museum itself, but there are also nice extras. For example, you can delve into one specific work of art in six steps. Or look together with your colleagues at the structures within your organization, using art of course. In any case, the workshop challenges you to look beyond the known and provides new insights that you share with the other students.

HISTORY

Nxt Museum, the first museum in the Netherlands for new media art, opens its doors on August 29, 2020. With a three-part program consisting of exhibitions, performances and education, Nxt Museum brings art and technology together with the focus on 'what is next'. In a former production studio of 2,100 square meters, the museum provides a unique addition to the flourishing creative district of Amsterdam-Noord. Nxt Museum is a new cultural destination for the city and surrounding areas. Inspired by the international museum landscape where interactive technology-driven exhibitions Nxt Museum wants to offer visitors a unique and often personalized experience. It wants to be the pioneer in the field of emerging tech, new media art and visitor experience in the Netherlands. The museum is designed to address contemporary social issues and get people thinking about issues surrounding people and technology. Nxt Museum exhibits large-scale art installations, which are developed through interdisciplinary collaborations between established and emerging artists, designers, technicians, musicians and scientists.
